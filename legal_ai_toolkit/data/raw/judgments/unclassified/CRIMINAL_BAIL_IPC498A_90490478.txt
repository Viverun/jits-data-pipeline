8.    Point   No.1:­  In   order   to  bring   home   the   guilt   of

accused,   the   prosecution   has   examined   PW.1.     PW1

who is the IO who has deposed that he has registered

the   crime   and   sent   FIR   to   the   court   and   for   further

investigation handed over case file to CW13.



9.    It is paramount fact to note that in the present

case on hand even after issuance of summons warrant

and proclamation to CW2, 3, 5 to 9. The prosecution

has failed to secure the presence of CW2, 3, 5 to 9. As

such by rejecting the prayer of Sr.APP the CW2, 3, 5 to

9   have   been   dropped   as   not   secured.   Since,   the

prosecution   has   not   examined   material   witness   i.e.,

complaint   and   mahazar   and   other   independent

witness.   Under   those   circumstances   the   prosecution

case falls short and based on the sole evidence of PW1

who has registered the crime, the guilty of the accused

cannot be proved. As such, the prosecution has failed

                                               6                                          C.C.17620/2008





to   prove   the   guilty   of   the   accused   CW2,   3,   5   to   9

beyond   all   reasonable   doubt.     Accordingly,   I   answer

point no.1 in the negative.
